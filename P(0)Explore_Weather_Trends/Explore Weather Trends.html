Insights & Observation¶

Similarities Global vs. My Local:

  1. Parallel Moving Average.
  2. The Up/Down slopes have similar trends and consistant.
  3. Global and Local average temperature is on the rise.

Differences Global vs. My Local:

  1. Based on the year of 1870-1900, the slopes appear to have an different trends towards one another.
  2. The AVG Temperature of local and global are different based on its scaling.

Observations:

  1. My local average temperature is hotter then average global temperature.
  2. The rate of change is consistant between the loval and global data.
  3. The overall trend shows that the world is getting warmer over time.
  4. Our world's down trend occured in the mid 1950 - 2000, stating that we did something for the toward trend to occur.
